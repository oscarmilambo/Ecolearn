Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with EcoLearn?

Simply click "Join Free" to create your account. You'll have immediate access to free courses and can start learning right away!

Is EcoLearn really free?

Yes! Most of our courses are completely free. We also offer premium content for those who want advanced certifications.

Can I access EcoLearn on my phone?

Absolutely! EcoLearn is fully mobile-responsive and works on any device - smartphone, tablet, or computer.

How can I report illegal dumping in my area?

Once registered, go to the "Report" section, take a photo of the dumping site, add location details, and submit. Authorities will be notified automatically.

Do you offer certificates?

Yes! Upon completing courses, you'll receive digital certificates that you can share on social media or add to your resume.
