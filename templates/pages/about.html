Our Mission

To empower every Zambian with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to build a sustainable future through accessible environmental education.

  • Provide free, quality environmental education
  • Foster community-driven environmental action
  • Bridge the gap between knowledge and practice

Our Vision

A Zambia where every community actively participates in environmental conservation, creating a greener, healthier nation for generations to come.

  • Zero waste communities across Zambia
  • Widespread adoption of renewable energy
  • Protected ecosystems and wildlife

Our Story

How EcoLearn came to be

EcoLearn was born from a simple observation: Zambia's environmental challenges require not just awareness, but action. Founded in 2024, we recognized that traditional environmental education wasn't reaching communities effectively.

We saw illegal dumping sites growing, rivers being polluted, and communities lacking the knowledge to make sustainable choices. But we also saw potential – passionate individuals ready to learn, communities eager to change, and a nation rich in natural beauty worth protecting.

Today, EcoLearn combines cutting-edge e-learning technology with local knowledge, making environmental education accessible in English, Bemba, and Nyanja. We're not just teaching – we're building a movement of environmental champions across Zambia.
